Karlos Lenniel

Founder & CEO

Karlos Lenniel is a 19-year-old visionary that puts his ideas in motion, first in paper and then into creations. Born and raised in Puerto Rico, a Caribbean island has impacted his outlook on a sustainable life.

Focused on innovation, his goal is to give environmental sustainability to people, so they can have access to essential resources. Currently, he’s studying Mechanical Engineering at University of Puerto Rico, Mayagüez Campus. As it is, achieving an inclusive world trough our company’s inventions is the dream he is shaping into a reality.
