WATRIC

At Watric Energy Resources, we are interested in everything that involves sustainability. Our mission is to achieve total sustainability in your home through the development of innovative products. We are a start-up focused on helping the world reach a sustainable future, with access to required resources and renewable energy.

Since we originate from Puerto Rico, a Caribbean island exposed to natural phenomena such as hurricanes and droughts, we are conscious of the importance of illumination, energy, and water. The life experience that our island provides us is our inspiration for our products in development.

This is what we have decided to concentrate on, not only on the collective sustainability but also on the individual one. Through our values, we aim to contribute to a sustainable world.

Karlos Lenniel

Our Founder

Karlos Lenniel is an 18-year-old visionary that puts his ideas in motion, first in paper and then into creations. Born and raised in Puerto Rico, a Caribbean island has impacted his outlook on a sustainable life.

A product of the public-school system, he’s on track to make renewable resources accessible for everyone. Focused on innovation, his goal is to give environmental sustainability to people, so they can have access to essential resources. Currently, he’s studying Mechanical Engineering at University of Puerto Rico, Mayagüez Campus. As it is, achieving an inclusive world through our company inventions it’s his ultimate dream.
