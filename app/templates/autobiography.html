About Us

Everything that involves sustainability is part of our interests. We are a start-up that wants to help the world achieve a sustainable future full of access to resources and renewable energy, by developing our concepts in creations.

Since our origins are from Puerto Rico, a Caribbean island exposed to natural phenomena, such as hurricanes and droughts, we are conscious of the importance of illumination, energy, and water.

This is how we decided to concentrate, not only on the collective sustainability but also on the individual one. The life experience that our island provides us is our inspiration for the products in growth. Through our values, we aim to contribute to a sustainable world.

Our Founder

Karlos Lenniel is an 18-year-old visionary that puts his ideas in motion, first in paper and then into creations. Born and raised in Puerto Rico, a Caribbean island has impacted his outlook on a sustainable life.

A product of the public-school system, he’s on track to make renewable resources accessible for everyone. Focused on innovation, his goal is to give environmental sustainability to people, so they can have access to essential resources. Currently, he’s studying Mechanical Engineering at University of Puerto Rico, Mayagüez Campus. As it is, achieving an inclusive world through our company inventions it’s his ultimate dream.
