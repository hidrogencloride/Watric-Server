Water

The Problem

With the demand for water increasing every year around 843 million people live without access to safe water. On the way the market of sustainability is growing drastically but it concentrates on energy leaving aside the development of sustainable water sources. Our purpose is to give water sustainability to each house and individual.

For the community and the world

Our Solution

Our team is working every day to achieve a water generator that can supply to your entire house making it sustainable on water and taking you away from water bills. We expect to achieve with our final model a water production of 100 gallons peer day so any home can collect up to 700 gallons in a week and run a full average house.

Water Directly From Air

How?

We are developing a technology where we achieve water production directly from air and free of air compression systems. Leading us to provide water production at the lowest energy cost and maintenance. With an autonomous system developed by us we are able to adapt to the external environment, ensuring the production of water in many places.

Walt's Purpose

WALT is our funding maker for the development of the full house cover water generator. While we improve our system of water production we decided to give the public a taste of what is to have water directly from air and the ability to produce it at home. Obtaining a WALT will give you a hint of what sustainability in water is and at the same time you support our development and the impulse for a better sustainable world.
