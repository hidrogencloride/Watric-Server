Water

The Problem

With the demand for water increasing every year, around 843 million people live without safe access to this essential resource. As it is, the market for sustainability is growing drastically, but it concentrates on energy, leaving aside the development of sustainable water sources. Our dedication is to give reliable water to everyone.

Our Solution

We are developing a technology where we produce water directly from air and free of air compression systems. Leading us to provide water at the lowest energy cost and maintenance. In such a way, with an autonomous system, we can adapt to the external environment by ensuring the production of water in numerous locations.

Water From Air

Producing water from air means that you won’t worry about buying water refill anymore. You have the option to produce your own water at home, which is cost-effective and sustainable. Let’s not forget that it will impact places with low access.

Water sustainability at home

Our team works to achieve a water generator that can supply an entire house. Water bills won’t be a problem anymore as we expect to achieve, with our final model, a water production of 100 gallons per day. It makes possible for any home to collect up to 700 gallons in a week and run a full average house.

Meet Walt

WALT is the drinking only compact version of our atmospherics water generators that will make drinkable water. It’ll be the water fountain that can be placed in your kitchen, backyard, terrace or office. Say goodbye to paying for water bottles or refill. Its scale of water production is 3 gallons per day, and if you want to increase the water production, you can always have more than one working together.

Walt's Purpose

WALT is our funding maker for the development of the full house cover water generator. While we improve our system of water production we decided to give the public a taste of what is to have water directly from air and the ability to produce it at home. Obtaining a WALT will give you a hint of what sustainability in water is and at the same time you support our development and the impulse for a better sustainable world.
