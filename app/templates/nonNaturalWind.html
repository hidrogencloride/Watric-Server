The development of this product is inspired by the problem of lighting the roads in our home, Puerto Rico. The island calculates an expenditure of $120 dollars per post, even documented in the year 2012 a cost of $112 million dollars annually. Our solution

With our main product, we focus on bringing environmental responsibility to the roads, thus elevating sustainability to another level. How do we accomplish this? Our Non-natural wind turbines works from the wind produced by the vehicles on the road when passing the turbine. In other words, the turbine produces energy based on traffic patterns during the day and night.

Non-Natural Wind Turbines

Non-natural wind turbines are more constant energy sources, unlike solar panels. This is how the product represents our goal as a company, to implement innovation in the world. We managed to exploit, rarely, a non-natural resource produced by human activity for the benefit of a sustainable world. The turbines are in development stage to perfect them at their maximum capacity.
