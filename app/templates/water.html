Water

With the demand for water increasing every year, around 843 million people live without safe access to this resource. The market for sustainability is growing drastically, but it concentrates on energy, leaving aside the development of sustainable water sources.

Our Solution

We are developing technology capable of producing water directly from the air and free of air compression systems, leading us to provide water at the lowest energy cost and maintenance. Using an autonomous system, we can adapt the system’s conditions to the external environment, thus ensuring the production of water in numerous locations.

Water From Air

Producing water from air means that you won’t worry about buying water refills anymore. You can produce your own water at home, which is cost effective.

Water sustainability at home

Our team works to design an atmospheric water generator that can supply an entire house. Water bills won’t be a problem anymore as we expect to achieve, with our final model, a production of 100 gallons per day. This makes it possible for any home to collect up to 700 gallons in one week and run a full average house.

Meet Walt

WALT is the drinking-only compact version of our atmospheric water generators that will produce potable drinking water. Water bottles or refills won’t be a problem anymore, as WALT is a fountain that can be placed in your kitchen, backyard or terrace. Its scale of production is 3 gallons per day. Who said you can’t have more than one working together though.
